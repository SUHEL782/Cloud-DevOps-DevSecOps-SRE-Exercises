# Contributing Guidelines

Thank you for considering contributing to **Cloud-DevOps-DevSecOps-SRE-Exercises**!  

This repository contains exercises and interview questions for **Cloud, DevOps, DevSecOps, and SRE**. We welcome contributions that improve the quality, clarity, and breadth of the content.

---

## Ways to Contribute

You can contribute in the following ways:

- Add new exercises or interview questions  
- Fix typos, formatting issues, or broken links  
- Improve explanations or examples  
- Update outdated information or resources  
- Suggest new topics or improvements  

---
